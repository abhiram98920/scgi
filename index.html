Building a Legacy of Healthcare Excellence

SCGI has scripted success stories in the field of education and was established in the year 2006. We are affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS). SCGI is nestled in Kolar with own hospital, situated just beside the Chennai–Bangalore highway.

At SCGI, we believe that education is not limited to just academics. We aim to teach the upcoming generations that learning and developing their individual skills are for providing quality care, responsibility and respect within the society.

Campus Life

Our Facilities

At SCGI, every facility is thoughtfully designed to support the holistic growth of our students — academically, clinically, and personally.

Own Hospital

Equipped with advanced medical equipment, providing students with real-world clinical exposure right on campus.

Hostel

Separate secure hostel for girls within the college premises and a separate hostel for boys — safe and comfortable.

Digital Classrooms

All classrooms are equipped with projectors and interactive boards for a modern and engaging learning environment.

Specialised Labs

Nursing Foundation, Maternity & Child Health, Community Health, Nutrition, Anatomy, Microbiology, Biochemistry & Computer labs.

Library & Research Centre

Adequate collection of library books and journals with internet facilities to support academic research.

Placement Cell

Placement guidance is provided and SCGI students are successfully placed in hospitals across India and abroad.

Arts & Culture

Various cultural activities are organised to enhance student skills, creativity, teamwork, and overall personality.

Trained Tutors

Multi-disciplinary programmes supported by experienced and dedicated teachers committed to student success.
