B.Sc Nursing

The purpose of the Basic B.Sc Nursing course is to prepare a qualified general nurse and midwife who will function as a member of a Health team, beginning with consultancy for the first level position in the hospital, community health services especially in rural areas and in education spectrum as a Lecturer.

Answer for Why B.Sc Nursing

A BSc Nursing course will train you to perform to the best of your abilities under different circumstances. It will also train you to nurse people of all age groups. But why should you choose a career in this field?

As per studies carried out in 2018, the value of the global healthcare industry was 8 trillion that year. In other words, healthcare alone forms 10% of GDP in some developed countries. Eligibility

10+2 class or its equivalent passed with aggregate of 45% marks in Science which consist of subjects Physics, Chemistry and Biology conducted by the department of pre-university education in anywhere in India. English Mandatory

Eligibility for Examination

Students should have 80% attendance each in theory and practical. The student should complete 11 months duration of that particular academic year before appearing for the examination.

The principal should certify that the candidate’s performance and conduct are satisfactory. If a student fails in one theory paper or practical examination, she/he may sit for a supplementary examination in the failed subjects or practical held subsequently.
